FAQ

How do I switch language?
QuickLedger automatically follows your device language setting. Change it in iOS Settings → Language & Region.

Is my data safe?
Yes. All financial data is stored locally on your device only. We never upload or access your data.

How do I set a custom API key?
Go to Settings → Gemini API Key, enter your key from aistudio.google.com.
