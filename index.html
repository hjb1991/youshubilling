Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

QuickLedger ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Data Collection and Storage

All financial data you enter (transactions, budgets, account information) is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal or financial data on external servers.

AI Features

Our AI-powered insights feature uses Google's Gemini API. When you request AI analysis, your spending data is sent to the Gemini API for processing. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests. You can use a custom API key in settings for added privacy.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with Google Gemini API for AI analysis features. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Permissions

Camera: Used only for OCR receipt scanning. Microphone: Used only for voice input. Neither is used for background recording.

No Advertising

We do not serve advertisements or use any ad networks.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.
