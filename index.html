Sor Quickledger — Privacy Policy

Developer: YoumiSingapore  |  Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sor Quickledger mobile application (the "App"), developed and provided by YoumiSingapore ("we", "our", or "the Developer").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information. All your accounting data (transaction records, categories, budgets, account information) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to any of your financial data.

2. Voice Recognition

The voice input feature uses your device's built-in speech recognition engine for on-device processing. We do not store or retain any of your voice recordings.

3. Camera & Photo Library

The receipt scanning feature accesses your camera or photo library only when you actively initiate it. Photos are used solely for on-device OCR text recognition and are never uploaded to any server.

4. Network Usage

Network access is used only for in-app AI text recognition requests. The App does not include any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or user behavior tracking components.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDK:

  • Google MLKit — for on-device OCR text recognition (no data transmitted to the cloud)

This service runs locally on your device and does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information.

6. Data Security

The App supports system-level biometric authentication (Fingerprint / Face ID) to protect your financial data from unauthorized access. We have no backend servers; all data exists solely on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
